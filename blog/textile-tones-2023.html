Textile Tones 2023

  • By J&J
  • July 9, 2023 - 5 min read

"You have to learn the rules of the game. And then you have to play better than anyone else."
- Albert Einstein

In the world of interior design, color and texture blend to create captivating spaces that resonate with the soul. As we step into the year 2023, new textile tones emerge, bringing fresh dimensions and sensations to interior environments. Let's explore the evolving trends and tones that are set to define the design landscape in this exciting era.

Rich, Earthy Berry Colors

The palette for 2023 showcases a deep reverence for nature's bounty. Expect to see more velvet textures, emphasizing comfort and tactile luxury. Curved shapes will be prominent, evoking a sense of softness and serenity. Alongside these indulgent shapes, brass and gold accents will glimmer, adding opulence and warmth to interiors.

Muted Pastels and Subdued Colors

Step back from vibrant hues, and embrace pastels with a twist. Muted pastels take center stage, inviting tranquility while maintaining a sense of modern sophistication. These colors whisper rather than shout, evoking a sense of calm and serenity. Imagine the pastels you know and love, then dial them down a notch, creating an atmosphere of subtle elegance.

Apricot Orange: The New Neutral

A fresh update on the Millennial pink trend, apricot orange emerges as the new neutral. This coral-blush color injects warmth and vibrancy while remaining versatile enough to complement a variety of design styles. Whether you incorporate it as an accent or a statement hue, apricot orange breathes new life into interiors.

As we venture into 2023, the language of textiles and colors continues to evolve. These textile tones go beyond mere aesthetics; they shape emotions, define spaces, and provide a canvas for personal expression. Embrace the richness of these colors, the subtlety of their hues, and the narratives they weave into your interior landscapes. Just as Albert Einstein suggested, in the world of design, learning the rules is only the beginning. It's in the artful application of these rules that we truly create spaces that resonate and inspire.

At Jensen & Juhl, we’re committed to transforming ideas into impactful realities. With expertise in design, production, and branding, our strategic partnerships create memorable experiences that connect brands with their audiences. Let’s build the future together.
